How it works: The Advisor compares teams in the 2014 tournament field to teams from the last 11 NCAA tournaments, and projects their chance of beating another team in a matchup.

Based on the way the Advisor operates, a team that fared better in one category than another will not necessarily be more likely to win. (For example, NCAA tournament teams ranked No. 2 in RPI have fared slightly better in the past 11 tournaments than teams ranked No. 1 in RPI.)
